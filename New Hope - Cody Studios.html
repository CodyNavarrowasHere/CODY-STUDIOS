Star Wars Episode 4
A New Hope
This Star Wars film was released May 25, 1977 and is the first Star Wars film. Created by George Lucas this film contained ionic characters such as Luke Skywalker, Princess Leia, Han Solo and Chewie. Star Wars A New Hope focuses on the Rebellions last chance to have a fighting chance against the Empire. This film takes place 15 years after the Revenge of the Sith and is the first Star Wars film creating the franchise. Luke Skywalker is the main antagonist in this film and he must set off with a mysterious hermit named Ben Kenobi to save a princess and send the ‘Death Star” plans to the Rebellion  after his Uncle and Aunt dies. Then gets thrown into the rebellion as a pilot and expected to help destroy the Empire’s super weapon “The Death Star”. After that Luke then stays with the Rebellion and manages to keep Han with him.

The Galactic Empire

The Imperial Empire was created at the ending of the Clone Wars and was ruled by Emperor Palpatine, who took over by deceiving the Senate and destroying the Jedi. He told the senate that the Jedi was corrupt and was planning on turning on the Republic to create their own order. All he needed was a setup, which he got when he revealed him to be Darth Sidious to the Anakin, who ran and told Mace Windu. Mace Windu then with 3 other Jedi went to arrest Palpatine and to show the Republic that he was a fraud, but instead the 4 Jedi went down and Anakin became Darth Vader. That was all Palpatine needed to convince the senate that the Jedi were betraying the Republic. He said that the Jedi attempted to assassinate him, so he ordered the Clone Army to wipe the Jedi out.
